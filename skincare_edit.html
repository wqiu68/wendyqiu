my skincare philosophy

I believe skincare should be fun but functional. While I enjoy trying new products, I've learned that a simple, consistent routine with evidence-backed ingredients works best for me. Everyone's skin is different, so what works for me might not work for you—but hopefully, these recommendations give you a good starting point!
