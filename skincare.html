Aquaphor Healing Ointment Baby Advanced Therapy

this is the BEST, ABSOLUTE BEST balm you can have for dry lips and skin. the formula is far best than any other ointment out there. if there's one thing you need to invest in, this is it. i religiously carried a small tub of this for 9 months and to this day, still only swear by this balm.

Vitamin E supplements

get yourself a bottle of Vitamin E because accutane is harsh on the body, which can cause hair loss and really sensitive skin. i think taking Vitamin E throughout the entire period was helpful in managing these side effects.

Fish Oil supplements

accutane sucks all the oil out your body so naturally you will be a very dry, scaly person. do yourself a favor and just take some fish oil to alleviate the side effects of that.

Claritin (or any allegery meds)

if you have allergies and are on accutane during the spring, TAKE ALLERGY MEDICINE!! my eyes would be bloodshot red if i didn't take this haha. it also helps with the purging period.

EAT FAT WITH YOUR ACCUTANE PILLS

to get the most effects out of the accutane capsules you take, make sure you take it with something fatty. accutane is absorbed best with fatty foods so to make each pill count, make sure you pair it with a fat. I typically just drank a cup a milk with the pills. Other options include peanut butter, avocado, nuts etc. also take the pills with your biggest meal.
